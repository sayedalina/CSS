Indian Cricket Legends


Virat Kohli

king of cricket

  • Born: 5 November 1988
  • Nickname: King Kohli
  • Role: Batsman
  • Batting Style: Right-handed
  • Former captain of the Indian cricket team
  • Known for aggressive batting and consistency
  • One of the highest run-scorers in international cricket

Rohit Sharma

Hitman of India

  • Born: 30 April 1987
  • Nickname: Hitman
  • Role: Batsman
  • Batting Style: Right-handed
  • Captain of the Indian cricket team
  • Holds the record for the highest individual ODI score (264)
  • Famous for elegant stroke play and big centuries

MS Dhoni

Captain Cool

  • 7 July 1981
  • Nickname: Captain Cool
  • Role: Wicket-keeper Batsman
  • Batting Style: Right-handed
  • Led India to multiple ICC trophies
  • Famous for calm leadership and helicopter shot
  • One of the greatest finishers in cricket history
